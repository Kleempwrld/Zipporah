About Me

My name is Elly Enga, from Mansa, Zambia. Life has been a journey filled with challenges, moments of loneliness, and learning how to make the most of what I have. My family had moderate financial resources, and I grew up understanding the value of patience, resilience, and hard work.

Since childhood, I have been fascinated by technology and learning. I would spend hours exploring computers, coding small programs, and imagining a life beyond my immediate circumstances. Even in lonely times, curiosity and determination kept me moving forward.

Through every challenge, there has been Zipporah, my best friend, my light, and the person I love deeply. She is cute, more than any girl I have ever met. Her love is constant, pure, and unwavering. She supports me, encourages me, and makes life brighter even when things seem dark. I want to be with her forever, and I don’t love girls randomly; my heart belongs only to Zipporah.

Life may be lonely sometimes, but with hope, love, and determination, I strive to create a brighter future. Every dream I have, every project I build, is fueled by the desire to make a life that both Zipporah and I can cherish together.

Education

I started school at Mutende Day Secondary School and later continued at Don Bosco Secondary School for Grades 10 to 12. Resources were limited, and life taught me the importance of dedication and self-motivation.

I spent countless nights studying mathematics, science, and programming. I also explored online tutorials to enhance my skills. Each accomplishment, no matter how small, reminded me that education opens doors to possibilities beyond my environment.

Career & Dreams

I want to become a software developer and build technology that helps people and improves lives. I am starting small, learning daily, and building projects step by step. Every achievement motivates me to keep growing and improving.

Additionally, I have a dream to become a pilot and fly for Emirates Airlines. Aviation has fascinated me since childhood, and the idea of traveling the world while doing what I love excites me deeply. I hope one day to combine my passion for technology with my dream of flying, creating opportunities to explore both the digital and real skies.

Zipporah’s love and support inspire me to continue pursuing my dreams, both in programming and aviation. Life may be challenging, but determination, love, and ambition guide me toward a bright future.
